Tech Consultant & Auditor

Provided consulting services to help companies and teams lead transformations and grow

Project Details

  • Client : Software companies
  • Location : France
  • Started on : September 2016
  • Duration : 2.5 years

Description

Provided consulting services to help tech companies of all sizes and industries grow and address strategic, operational and technical challenges.

It always started by participating in the company’s day-to-day activities and interacting with all its stakeholders. The idea was to adopt a systemic approach, analysing the organisation’s state through different perspectives, to gain a deeper understanding of its needs.

Achievements

  • • Helped 10+ tech companies build high-performing teams, deliver high-quality, customer-focused solutions and achieve diverse strategic goals (e.g. spark innovation, improve performance, expand).

  • • Designed and executed relevant actionable plans.

  • • Broke silos between departments and successfully enabled company-wide changes by interacting with all relevant stakeholders (e.g. leadership, engineering, product and sales teams).

  • • Managed constraints and optimised processes successfully, lowering project cycle times and increasing dynamic capabilities and customer satisfaction.

  • • Played a key role in aligning development teams, technical decision-makers, managers, business executives and other interested parties.

  • • Coached, mentored and supported 15+ engineering teams, resulting in higher performance, software excellence and increased motivation.

  • • Audited 5+ software systems and provided concrete and actionable recommendations to improve the code, architecture and development process.

  • • Delivered new value to internal and external stakeholders.

  • • Hosted 15+ events and facilitated workshops to enable knowledge sharing.
