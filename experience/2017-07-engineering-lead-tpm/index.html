Senior Technical Program Manager • Engineering Lead

Helped a tech company open new markets in Europe

Project Details

  • Client Type : Startup
  • Industry : Software
  • Location : France
  • Started on : July 2017
  • Duration : 1 year

Description

Led the transformation of a firm’s cloud platform (~40 people). It was critical for the company’s business strategy as it allowed the opening of new markets in Europe.

Achievements

  • • Doubled the team’s long-term velocity, without compromising quality, by providing support for the entire team, fostering knowledge sharing, addressing functional and technical bottlenecks as well as introducing a more Agile and engaged mindset.

  • • Helped define a roadmap to ensure product-market fit.

  • • Ensured strategic alignment across departments.

  • • Set up a software delivery process which paved the way for the company to replace its legacy platform with this new one. This process successfully underwent an audit from EY.

  • • Reduced time-to-market to gain traction among the new customer base.
