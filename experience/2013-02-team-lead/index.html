Led the qualification of a tech project for BioMérieux

Project Details

  • Client Type : Large enterprise
  • Industry : Biotechnology
  • Location : France
  • Started on : February 2013
  • Duration : 1 year 2 months

Description

Led the qualification of a BioMérieux software project aiming to enable the validation and release of medical reagents batches at the end of the production chain (10 people team).

Achievements
